At Small Robot Co, our ultimate ambition is to help farmers understand and optimise care for every plant in every field, everywhere. We call it Per Plant Farming and it will maximise food production while reducing its cost on the environment. You can join us on this journey.

1. Small is sustainable

Cereal farming has become unsustainable, unprofitable and a global food security risk. With up to 90% of chemicals wasted, Small Robot Co offers farmers a revolutionary new system and could exponentially cut chemicals, emissions and improve biodiversity. Small Robot Co has just been chosen to be part of the PWC/Tech Nation 2022 Net Zero X, an exclusive network of high-growth UK tech companies focused on reducing global emissions.

2. Small is good

Treatment maps created with data from our Tom robot can save up to 90% herbicide and estimated 15% fertiliser, delivering significant value to farmers and the environment. Our sprayer integration is partnered with Tuckwells, one of the largest John Deere dealers in the UK. This is just the beginning.

3. Small is a service

Our robots are now live on UK farms and we have reached max service capacity for 2022-23. With 100 EOIs for 50,000+ hectares for potential future service, we are on track to secure £3m of sales.

4. Small is smart

Our Per Plant data is enormously compelling to the Corporate Agri-science industry. We have 5 pending corporate trials including James Hutton Institute & Momentive. The data Tom collects can model the behaviour of a plant throughout the season. This creates a virtual testing process - a digital twin of the field. 

5. Small is modular

One platform, multiple applications. Our new modular platform enables intelligence and action applications to share 80% of the hardware, 90% of the electronics and all of the deployed software. This platform will allow us to scale internationally to cover much larger farms.

6. Small is scalable

We are planning our Canada go-to-market: we have EOIs with initial farmers, including Terry Aberhart and Chuck Baresich. We are exploring funded trials starting with Saskatchewan and Ontario for 2023-2024. It is this expansion that will grow us into the multi-billion dollar opportunity.

7. Small is secure

We are patent protected in the UK, with pending applications internationally. With over £2million in Innovate UK grants to date, grants from the British government have underpinned much of our early stage R&D work, helping us prove the concepts behind our technology and determine the best path to commercialisation. 

8. Small is skilled

Our strong mission and “tech for good” focus has been a key factor in recruiting and retaining an excellent team, which we see as an accumulating advantage. Our team is cherry picked from leading tech, engineering and AI companies, including Google, JCB, Syngenta, Haas F1, Hummingbird, and Ordnance Survey.

9. Small is celebrated

We have just been shortlisted for the inaugural New Statesman Positive Impact Awards. The Grocer tipped us as an agtech trailblazer and Business Insider includes us in a list of most promising climate-tech startups according to top VCs. Tech media highlights include the Financial Times, WIRED, The Guardian, CNN and The Economist.

10. Small is for farmers

Right from the beginning, we’ve co-designed with farmers, to ensure we are closely answering our customers’ needs. Our Advisory Group and customer sign ups include many farming influencers and industry leaders. By farmers, for farmers.
